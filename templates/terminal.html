Our Favorite Way To Order Coffee

Some of our earliest memories together are exploring Winston-Salem on bike and foot to visit a variety of different coffee shops. We've bonded over enjoying coffee together in the mornings, especially when it was paired with an activity that we both love.

Now, as two aspiring computer nerds, Terminal.shop is our favorite way to order coffee for home enjoyment. We were super excited to be able to order coffee straight from the command line! This may not be as exciting to everyone else, but we're absolutely thrilled! Check them out with: 

$ ssh terminal.shop

Or, for those of you afraid of the command line, visit terminal.shop
