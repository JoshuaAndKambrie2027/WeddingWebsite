Our Story

He went from avoiding social events to a full-blown social butterfly… all because he had to talk to one girl. Funny how love works.

How We Met

It all started when he was forced to talk to me on my second day of work. Don't worry, nothing really happend other than Joshua saying words that I didn't understand, and I pretended that I had everything under-control. But, on my third day of work, he found out I liked to bike and I found out he printed a conference poster with a single red period on it (not like I would poke fun at him or anything over that). Our friend Andrea very causually asked if I wanted to go see the bike room with them and on the way out, Andrea left us and Joshua asked me if I wanted to bike with them one day that week. He then asked for my phone number so he could "create a group chat" for the bike ride. Yes, he is that smooth, never let him convince you otherwise.

Our Adventures

Ever since that day, we have shared adventures big and small: biking in the rain, backpacking in national parks, walking around Winston-Salem, and long road trips. Each one has been full of laughter and the joy of exploring new places together.

The Proposal

On a serene beach at sunset…just kidding. The ring? Handed to me in bed. The mountain photos? A 4-mile hike the next day, purely for appearances. Not like anyone would expect anything different from us.
