Explore Boone, North Carolina

We're so excited to celebrate with you! If you have extra time during your stay, here are some popular ways to experience the mountains, food, and charm of Boone.

Outdoor Adventures

Scenic

Blue Ridge Parkway

One of the most beautiful drives in the country, with overlooks, hiking trails, and sweeping mountain views just minutes from Boone.

Hiking

Nearby Trails

From gentle walks to challenging hikes, the High Country offers trails for every pace and skill level.

Adventure

Ziplining & Aerial Parks

Try a zipline or ropes course nearby for a fun, adrenaline-filled way to see the forest from above.

History & Culture

Local History

Hickory Ridge History Museum

Explore historic cabins and learn about early Appalachian settlers and daily life in the mountains.

Explore

Self Guided Walking Tour

Take a walk through downtown Boone as fast or as slow as you want in this self-paced and self-guided tour.

Gardens

Daniel Boone Native Gardens

Peaceful walking paths surrounded by native plants, flowers, and historic structures.

Food & Drinks

Local Favorite

The Local

A fun, lively spot known for comfort food, cocktails, and a welcoming mountain-town vibe.

Local Food and Wine

Vidalia Wine & Kitchen

A relaxed wine bar offering seasonal dishes and a cozy atmosphere.

Coffee & Brunch

Local Cafés

Perfect for a slow morning—grab coffee, biscuits, or a light brunch before exploring town.

Downtown Boone

Strolling

King Street

Walkable downtown area filled with local shops, galleries, bookstores, and cafés.

Local Goods

Farmers Market

Explore the local farmers market and enjoy fresh produce, baked goods, and handmade crafts from local vendors.
